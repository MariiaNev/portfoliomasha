About my life. My achievements.

I’m a Product Designer with over five years of experience in UI/UX design.

After moving to New Zealand in 2023, I pursued further education at Media Design School, completing the GDST in Interactive Design to deepen my research and product design expertise. Today, I’m driven by continuous growth and excited to take on new challenges in product design.
