Multi-Role Education Platform

2023
Project overview

The project focused on developing an Education Management Platform designed to support internal educational processes across multiple institutions in Russia. System analysis revealed a high level of complexity due to numerous interconnected workflows and a hierarchical structure of user roles, each with different access levels and responsibilities. This required carefully structured interfaces and clear logic to support efficient day-to-day operations.

In response, the platform was refined as a scalable, multi-role system supporting student enrollment, application and contract management, instructor workflows, reinstatement and transfer processes, user management, and profile settings. My role involved designing and improving interfaces across multiple modules while ensuring consistency and smooth integration between them. The result is a clearer and more efficient internal platform that simplifies complex workflows and improves usability for staff working across different educational organisations.
